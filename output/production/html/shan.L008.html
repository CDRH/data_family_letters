  Ft Lupton Colo Jan 1.- 64. Mi querida hija

la precente es para saludar los, á todos:

Esperando que aygan pasado unos muy buenos, Cristmas: pues nosotros estubimos muy agusto esta noche pasamos con mi comadre Naty;

isimos una muy buena sena, y despues nos fuimos á la misa de amedia noche recibí Cristmas de Jessee y de Helen me dieron unos zapatos y un delantar y tambien de la Betty Baca me mando una cristmas Card, y $5.00 y tambien de Uds, que mandaron, pues á max si le gusto las uniones que le   mandaste; bueno tambíen a mi; muchas gracias por todo te platicare, que este dia es año new, y aqui esta tan boni dia mucho sol,

para el de cristmas si abia mucha, nieve; ó la Frances le dio mucho gusto por lo que le mandastes, dice que muchas Gracias;

bueno pues ya no tengo mas nuevas. yo creo que esto sera todo, por esta ves, y tambíen te quiero desir, que para cuidas vebies ajenos, yo creo que tu tienes sufuisienta trabajo en tu casa, y tu no tienes mucha necesidad; de batayar con familia ajena; yo creo que por eso estas tan flaca, cuidate porque si tu no te cuidas pos quien esperas que te cuide; mas por eso ni tienes tiempo ni de escribirme;

 

bueno Diosito que te ayude muchos recuerdos para todos dile al Roberto que me escriba, y la Pamela tambíen ó al Billy boy que me escriba pues yo me siento mal cuando no se nada de uds; bueno recíve el mas cincero recuerdos de nostostros te deceo un feliz año new y prosperidad, tu mamá de deceo Diosito que de tu

Bendiciones, Jesusita B. Torres

Translation

  Ft. Lupton, Colorado January 1. -64. My dear daughter

this letter is to greet everyone;

wishing that you all spent a very good Christmas; well we were very happy, we spent the evening with my comadre, Naty.

We made a very good supper, and afterwards we went to midnight mass. I received a Christmas presentfrom Jesse and from Helen they the gave me a pair of shoes and an apron, and also from Betty Baca, who sent me a Christmas card and $5.00 and also the things that you all sent me. Well Max liked the long johns that you

 

sent him. I did too. Thank you so much for everything. I will tell you that today is New Years and it’s a pretty day with a lot of sun,

but for Christmas there was a lot of snow. Frances really liked what you sent her. She said thank you very much;

Well, I don’t have much news. I think that’s all now. And also want to tell you why do you take care of other people’s babies, I think that you have enough work at home and you don’t have much need to struggle with other people’s families; I think this is why you are so thin. Take care of yourself, because if you don’t, who do you expect will. What’s more, I think this is why you don’t have time to write to me.

 

Well, God will help you. Many regards to all. Tell Roberto to write me, and Pamela too, and tell Billy Boy to write me because I feel bad when I hear nothing from you all. Well, receive the most sincere warmest regards from us. I wish for you a happy new year and prosperity, your mom who wishes you God’s blessings.

Jesusita B. Torres
