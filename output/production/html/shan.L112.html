Translation

  2. page

to the state of Texas, with a family that went there;

Well I had promised to visit the virgin of San Juan Texas a long time ago. It’s such a pretty church; I’m sending you a card and it’s just like it appears here. It’s a very hot place, lots of orange orchards. And I also saw of a lot of, well, banana plants or banana trees. And we crossed to Mexico in the car. We went in the morning and returned in the afternoon, for a day, to that place they call Reynosa, Mexico. Well, that place, Reynosa, didn’t seem so pretty to me, the people were very poor, the little adobe houses, very small
