  Huapamacato Mich Nov 30 de 1948 Sr. Maximino Torrez California Estimado Hermano

Contesto tú carta en la que me dises estas vien, Hermano me dispensas que no te havia contestado tu carta por motiva a que nó te imajinas la pena que pase de que un niño Alberto que es el mayor se dio un Balazo en una mano y queria avisarte asta que sanara, pera ahora ya parese que va de alibio Hermano y tamvien mi esposa siguio mas mala todavia del susto que paso. Hermano de lo que me dises del dinero pues el dia que dios te socorra me lo mandas como puedas y tú creas que salga mejor, y ahora díos me esta mandando tanta pena para aber si sufro con pasiensia y por ahora es cuanto resive saludos de mi esposa y familia para los tullos

tú hermano Clemente Torrez

Translation

  Huapamacato, Michoacán November 30, 1948 Mr. Maximino Torrez California Dear Brother,

I reply to your letter in which you tell me that you are well. Brother, please forgive my delay in answering your letter. I had not answered because you can’t even imagine the pain I’ve been through how worried I've been. My boy Alberto, the oldest, shot himself in the hand. I didn’t want to tell you until he healed, but now it looks like he is getting better, brother, and also, my wife got worse after such as scare. Brother, about the money you speak of, send it when God helps you, and in the way you see fit. God is now sending me so many tribulations to see if I can endure them with patience. For now this is all. Receive regards from my wife and family for all of yours.

Your brother Clemente Torrez
